HIRE ME

My Abilities

As a huge animal lover, I’ve wanted every type of pet imaginable throughout my life, rodents included. I vividly remember wearing gloves when I was a kid while handling hamsters because I didn’t want them to bite me. Now I have the ability to communicate and control an army of rats. I got an in-depth knowledge of Gotham's sewer system and the layout of the Blackgate Penitentiary, which comes in handy as I am able to smuggle items in and out of prison with the help of my little servants.


MY REPORTS

My Story


Becoming Ratcatcher II

I was born in Portugal. Despite his drug problems, my father loved me so much and taught me to use his gadgets to control rats, getting the animals to help and protect me whenever I need it. My father's drug problems kept growing and he died of an overdose. I lived on the streets with the rats and one day, I traveled to the United States to live the "American dream". But somehow I ended up following in the Ratcatcher's footsteps as a criminal, became Ratcatcher II.

The Suicide Squad

Cazo was caught by local law enforcement after robbing a bank and sent to Belle Reve. In that hellhole, I became a member of the second Task Force X strike team sent to Corto Maltese. There was so many things do, so many people to kill. I met so many famous supervillains including Bloodsport, Harley Quinn, King Shark. I played a major role in Starro's defeat by calling all the rats in the city to enter in the alien monster's eye and eat its organs, killing it.
